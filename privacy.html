1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Seylo (“we”, “us”, or “our”) handles information when you use the Seylo mobile application (“App”). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about what data the App uses and why.

App name: Seylo
Developer: Marcin Sobczyński
Contact: contact@seylo.app

2. Information We Collect

The App collects only the minimum data required to provide its core functionality:

  • Anonymous user identifier: When you first open the App, an anonymous account is created automatically. No name, email address, or personal profile is required or stored.
  • GPS / location data: The App accesses your device’s GPS position to look up the nearest aviation weather station (METAR) and to record position in logbook entries. Location data is used in real-time and stored only as part of the logbook entries you create.
  • Logbook entries: Data you enter yourself — such as vessel name, crew names, heading, speed, barometer readings, and notes — is stored locally on your device and optionally synced to a secure cloud backend.
  • Device information: Basic device metadata (Android version, app version) is used solely for delivering in-app updates and diagnosing compatibility issues. This data is not tied to your identity.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, payment information, advertising identifiers, or browsing history. We do not use analytics or advertising SDKs.

3. How We Use Information

  • To store and display your logbook entries within the App.
  • To fetch the nearest METAR weather report based on your current GPS position.
  • To sync your logbook data between devices via our cloud backend.
  • To deliver in-app update notifications.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Data may be processed by the following service providers solely to operate the App:

  • Supabase (privacy policy) – cloud database and anonymous authentication. Your logbook data is stored on Supabase-hosted PostgreSQL servers within the EU.
  • NOAA / aviationweather.gov – public weather API. The App sends your GPS coordinates to retrieve the nearest METAR report. No user account data is transmitted.

5. Android Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET – required to sync logbook data and fetch weather reports.
  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION – required to determine your position for METAR lookup and logbook entry recording.
  • ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION – required to record GPS position for automatic hourly watch entries when the screen is off.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED – required to restart the automatic watch timer after the device reboots.
  • WAKE_LOCK – required to keep the processor awake long enough to complete background processing.
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM / USE_EXACT_ALARM – required to schedule precise hourly watch reminders so entries are created at exact times.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE / FOREGROUND_SERVICE_DATA_SYNC – required on Android 12+ to run a foreground service for background data sync.
  • REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS – required to ensure background alarms are not deferred by manufacturer power managers.
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 9 and below only) – required to save exported PDF files to the device.

6. Data Storage and Security

All logbook data is stored locally on your device using an encrypted SQLite database. When an internet connection is available, data is synced to Supabase cloud servers protected by industry-standard TLS encryption in transit and AES-256 encryption at rest.

Your anonymous account is secured using Supabase’s authentication service. No passwords are stored on our servers.

7. User Rights and Data Deletion

Because the App uses anonymous authentication, all data belongs exclusively to you. You can:

  • Delete all local data by uninstalling the App from your device.
  • Request deletion of all cloud-stored data by contacting us at contact@seylo.app.

We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data through the App, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
